What does Vault protect?

Time

Expired or superseded memories should not keep winning retrieval.

Access

Private observations must not leak into shared answers because they are semantically similar.

Lifecycle

Deletion, TTL, review, and rollback retain auditable state.

Provenance

Results carry traceable sources instead of becoming unsupported text.
